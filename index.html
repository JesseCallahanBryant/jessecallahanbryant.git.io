About

I am a PhD student in sociology studying culture, philosophy, and the environment, with a research focus on right‑wing movements and computational text analysis. My dissertation examines how modern ideas of “nature” shape moral reasoning and social knowledge.

Interests: environmental sociology, computational social science, discourse & frame analysis, natural law, complex systems.
